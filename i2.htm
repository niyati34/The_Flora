Welcome To Abiya Gardens

Since our inception in 2018, we have steadily grown to become one of the largest suppliers of plants, seeds, pots, fertilizers, and landscaping services in Kerala. Our nursery is situated in 1 acre of easily accessible area at Kariyad, Nedumbassery, Kerala. As one of the largest wholesale nurseries in Kerala, Abiya Garden has a very large collection of wide varieties of all types of exotic fruits, Indian fruits, hybrid and normal coconut plants, medicinal plants, flowering plants, indoor-outdoor plants, landscaping plants, bonsai, succulents, cactus, hanging plants, orchids, palm trees, ornamental plants, creepers, shrubs, all types of pots, fertilizers, etc
